The "Scholar-Practitioner" Approach

My background bridges Educational Psychology and Digital Media.

As the founder of SISFIT, I hit a "Practitioner's Ceiling": I knew what content got views, but I lacked the rigorous evidence to prove why certain designs drove healthy behaviors while others caused anxiety.

My methodology combines Natural Language Processing (NLP) with Randomised Encouragement Trials (RET) to reverse-engineer the "Engagement vs. Health" paradox.
